This program is meant to light up all red pins whenever it's cold and dark- like a ghost detector (ooOOo).

![ghost](https://alechkana.github.io/assets/img/ghost.jpg)

**Tip**
If you've accidentally severed the connection between the main lilypad and the led pins, you can use the conductive thread to connect the points again!
