This program is meant to play a song (in this case, CBAT by Hudson Mohawke) through the lilypad buzzer speaker when the switch is on, and shut off when the switch is off.

There's not much to see on the arduino kit, but you can bet your bottom dollar that it's playing music.

![jammin](https://alechkana.github.io/assets/img/jammin.jpg)


**Tip:**
It really helps to not do this assignment in a room with other people (playing this song on loop gets old real fast).
